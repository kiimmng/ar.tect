Our story

Founded 2003 in Vietnam, ar.tech is a global architecture company that has grown to include 500 employees working out of a combined 7 offices around the world.

Since 2003 we work on a large variety of projects. We gained experience in the realization of architectural projects, renovations, interior designs and product designs. Our clients include property developers, housing corporations, multinational corporations, law firms and private families. We are ambitious and motivated. We like to learn and broadon our horizon.
